Will Rowan

Computer vision researcher and freelance film critic. I work on video foundation models and world models for the creative industries, finding ways to democratise Hollywood-grade VFX tools for productions of any budget.

PhD from the University of York, with publications at ICLR and ECCV. One of 200 filmmakers selected for Berlinale Talents 2025, and simultaneously one of 8 critics worldwide for Talents Press. Co-founder and CEO of PXLD, a University of York spinout building the next generation of filmmaking tools, featured in Variety and Screen Daily.
