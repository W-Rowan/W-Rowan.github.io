Positioning Principles Applied

No unexplained acronyms

ICVSS, ECCV, FEFF, EFM, CVMP — all spelled out or contextualised on first use

Stats lead, names follow

"1 of 8 critics worldwide" before "Berlinale Talents Press." The number is the hook.

Context for non-insiders

"Radu Jude (Golden Bear winner)" not just "Radu Jude." "dock10 (UK's largest TV facility)" not just "dock10."
