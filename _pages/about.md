Computer vision researcher and freelance film critic. I work on video foundation models and world models for the creative industries, finding ways to make powerful AI tools work for filmmakers and artists.

PhD from the University of York, with publications at ICLR and ECCV. One of 200 filmmakers selected for Berlinale Talents 2025, and simultaneously one of 8 critics worldwide for Talents Press. Currently a Research Fellow developing AI-powered virtual production tools with [dock10](https://www.dock10.co.uk) (the UK's largest television studio facility) as part of a £1.8M Innovate UK project, and co-founder of [PXLD](https://pxld.ai), a virtual production startup featured in Variety and Screen Daily.
